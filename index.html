The Artist Behind the Brush

"I paint to capture the fleeting moments of emotion that words cannot express."

Senora grew up surrounded by the vivid colors of the West Michigan coast, an influence that permeates every canvas she touches. What started as a therapeutic escape from the corporate world quickly evolved into a passionate career.

Her process is intuitive and physical. She often abandons brushes in favor of palette knives, sponges, and her own hands to manipulate the acrylics, building layer upon layer of texture.

Today, her work hangs in modern homes and offices across the globe, bringing energy, conversation, and a touch of organized chaos to every wall.
